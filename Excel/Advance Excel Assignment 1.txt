Advance Excel Assignment 1
 
1. What do you mean by cells in an excel sheet
A cell is a rectangle which is an intersection of a row and a column

2. How can you restrict someone from copying a cell from your worksheet
i. Right click the cell and click on format cells
ii. Go to Protection and ensure checkmark is present in Locked
iii. In the Menu Bar go to Review Tab and click on Protect Sheet
iv. Untick Select locked cells and set a password
v. Reenter password to confirm

3. How to move or copy the worksheet into another workbook
i. Right Click the sheet you want to move or copy
ii. Select Move or Copy
iii. If you want to move the worksheet into another workbook simply select the target workbook in the "To book" dropdown list and click OK
iv. If you want to copy the worksheet into another workbook ensure a checkmark is present in "Create a copy" and simply select the workbook in the "To book" dropdown list and click OK

4. Which key is used as a shortcut for opening a new window document
CTRL + N

6. When to use a relative cell reference in excel
 Relative cell reference is useful when the formula or calculation needs to be repeated accross multiple cells
