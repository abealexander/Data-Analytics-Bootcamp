1. How many types of conditions are available in conditional formatting on Excel
Following are some of the types of conditions available in conditional formatting on Excel:
Greater than
Less than
Between
Equal To
Text that Contains
A Date Cccurring
Duplicate Values
Top Bottom Rules

2. How to insert border in Excel with Format Cells dialog
i. In the format cells dialog box go to Border Tab
ii. Under the Border Tab select the Line Style, Colors or choose one of the borders from the presets and click OK

3. How to Format Numbers as Currency in Excel
Under the Home Tab, Number, Click on the Dropdown and select Currency

4. What are the steps to format numbers in Excel with the Percent style
Under the Home Tab, Number, Click on the Percentage (%) icon

5. What is a shortcut to merge two or more cells in excel
By selecting the cells you want to merge and clicking the following in sequence
ALT + H + M + M

6. How do you use text commands in Excel 
i. Select a Cell
ii. Type the equal (=) sign
iii. And enter the required text command

=TEXT(Value you want to format, "Format code you want to apply")
